• These are uncurated images from Unsplash.


    This project was built using simple HTML, CSS, and Unsplash's Source API.


    Inspired by Penelope Umbrico's Sunset Portraits from Sunset Pictures on Flickr, this piece explores the loss of individuality in the density of the Internet image-space. While Umbrico's work is finely curated to identify similar assertions, this piece takes advantage of Unsplash's plentiful collection and algorithmic randomization to reach a similar end: the wide range of subject matter strips all individual meaning through their juxtaposition, creating a collage of photos devoid of connection and intent.


    Users are encouraged to use the New Images button to generate a new piece.


    All photo credits go to the original authors (whoever they may be).
